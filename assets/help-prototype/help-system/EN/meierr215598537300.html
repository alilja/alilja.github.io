Send device data and reports to the Paceart Optima system

If your clinic uses a Paceart Optima system, the SessionSync feature enables you to transfer device data and reports to that system without ending the patient session:

  1. Tap , then tap SESSION > SessionSync > TRANSFER.

  2. To close the message, tap OK.
