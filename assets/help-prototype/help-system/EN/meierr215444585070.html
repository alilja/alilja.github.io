Use the Holter feature

To navigate quickly along a waveform trace, use the Holter feature:

  1. Open the FROZEN STRIP window.

  2. To select the waveform trace that you want to view, tap  on the top waveform source list and select a source.

  3. Tap HOLTER.

    The blue rectangle indicates the section of the waveform trace that is displayed in the FROZEN STRIP window. A green rectangle indicates a waveform strip.

  4. Use the following options:

    • To navigate along the waveform trace, tap  and .

    • Tap an area of the waveform trace to display that area in the FROZEN STRIP window.
