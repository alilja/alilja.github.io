View CareAlert and OptiVol event logs

View the logs of CareAlert EVENTS and OptiVol EVENTS that are stored in the implanted device:

  1. Tap , then tap DATA > CareAlert EVENTS.

  2. On the Medtronic CareAlert EVENTS window, tap either CareAlert EVENTS or OptiVol EVENTS.

  3. To create a report, tap the PDF button.
