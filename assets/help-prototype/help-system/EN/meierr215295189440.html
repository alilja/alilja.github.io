Draw notes

To annotate the waveform strip, draw notes on the waveform strip. If you generate a report of the strip, the notes that you draw on the waveform strip are included in the report.

  1. From the FROZEN STRIP window, tap .

  2. Draw on the waveform strip.

  3. Optionally, tap  and  to remove or reinsert drawings.

To disable the drawing mode, tap .
