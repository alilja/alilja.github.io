End the patient session

When you finish with the patient session, end the session.

Note:

After 45 min of inactivity, the implantable device app displays a message that prompts you to extend or end the session. If you do not respond to the message and there is 60 min of inactivity, the patient session ends automatically.

  1. Tap , then tap END SESSION.

  2. On the END SESSION? window, tap END NOW.
